Length Conversion Guide

Metric vs. Imperial Systems

The world primarily uses two systems for measuring length. Understanding the difference is key to accurate conversion.

Metric System (SI)

Used by 95% of the world. It is a decimal system based on powers of 10.

  • Base unit: Meter (m)
  • 1 km = 1,000 m
  • 1 m = 100 cm = 1,000 mm
Imperial / US Customary

Primarily used in the United States, Liberia, and Myanmar. Based on historical standards.

  • Base units: Inch, Foot, Yard, Mile
  • 1 ft = 12 in
  • 1 yd = 3 ft
  • 1 mi = 5,280 ft

Did You Know?

  • The Meter was originally defined in 1793 as one ten-millionth of the distance from the equator to the North Pole.
  • A Nautical Mile is based on the circumference of the earth and equals one minute of latitude (approx 1.15 regular miles).
